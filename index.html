Join Our Model Railway Community

Welcome to the Solid Fishstick Model Railway Club! We are a friendly community of model railway enthusiasts dedicated to the hobby of model railroading.

What We Offer

  • Weekly club meetings every Saturday at 10:00 AM
  • Large scale model railway layouts
  • Expert guidance for beginners
  • Trading and selling of model railway equipment
  • Annual railway exhibition and open days
  • Workshops on scenery building and locomotive maintenance

Scales We Work With

  • N Scale (1:160)
  • HO Scale (1:87)
  • O Scale (1:48)
  • G Scale (1:22.5)
