Our History

The Solid Fishstick Model Railway Club was founded in 1987 by a group of passionate model railway enthusiasts. What started as a small gathering of friends in a garage has grown into one of the region's premier model railway clubs.

Over the years, we have built several award-winning layouts and have been featured in Model Railway Magazine multiple times. Our club has grown to over 50 active members ranging from young beginners to seasoned experts with decades of experience.

Our Mission

To promote and preserve the hobby of model railroading through:

  • Education and skill sharing among members
  • Building and maintaining high-quality model railway layouts
  • Organizing public exhibitions to share our passion
  • Supporting newcomers to the hobby
  • Preserving railway history and heritage

Club Facilities

Main Clubhouse

Our 2,500 square foot clubhouse features:

  • Large HO scale modular layout with multiple operating levels
  • N scale switching layout for operations training
  • Workshop area with tools and workbenches
  • Library with over 200 railway books and magazines
  • Meeting room for presentations and social events
  • Kitchen facilities for club events

Outdoor Garden Railway

Weather permitting, members can operate trains on our G scale garden railway featuring:

  • Over 300 feet of track
  • Realistic mountain scenery
  • Multiple bridges and tunnels
  • Working signals and crossing gates

How to Join

Interested in joining our club? Here's how to get started:

  1. Attend one of our Open House events to meet members and see our facilities
  2. Complete a membership application form
  3. Pay annual dues
  4. Attend new member orientation session
  5. Start enjoying the hobby with fellow enthusiasts!
